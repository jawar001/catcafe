Our Story

Purr & Pour was born out of a simple idea: bringing comfort, coffee, and cats together. We partner with local shelters to provide a loving foster home to our furry residents until they find their forever families.

Our cafe is designed to be a safe haven for both our guests and our cats. Soft pastel colors, warm lighting, and cozy nooks make it the perfect place to decompress.
